PROJECTS

Project 1

Pagani Huayra

In the First year of Engineering, I got interested in 3D modelling, design and CGI. I modelled Pagani Huayra in First-year itself. I got to learn a lot while modelling that car. 30 hours of work culminated into something good. As an amateur, I was pretty satisfied with my work. But then I realised, there is so much more to explore!

Project 2

Nissan GT- R


After my first project, I was even more thrilled to try out new things in Maya and explore more rigging, texturing, etc., which I newly heard. I am an automotive enthusiast, and GT-R is one of my favourites, so I tried out a simple geometry to cut rendering time. This time, the focus was on learning more, not detailed modelling. I was pretty satisfied to learn about these concepts.
PS: Ignore the Arnold watermark in the video; I use the student version.
